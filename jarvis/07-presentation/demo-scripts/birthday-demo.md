# Birthday Demo Script — May 14th

## Setup (Before She Arrives)

1. JARVIS powered on, enclosure glowing idle blue
2. Bluetooth speaker charged and connected
3. Wipro lights on but dim/neutral
4. Room otherwise dark-ish
5. You've tested all commands within the last hour

---

## The Reveal

**Step 1: Activate**
> You: "Hey Jarvis"
> *LED ring pulses blue. NeoPixel strip brightens.*

**Step 2: Set the mood**
> You: "Set the lights to romantic mode"
> *Room transforms — warm pink/purple glow*
> JARVIS: "Going romantic. The room looks beautiful."

**Step 3: Music**
> You: "Play [her favorite song]"
> *Music starts from Bluetooth speaker*
> JARVIS: "Playing [song]. Perfect choice for tonight."

**Step 4: The birthday moment**
> You: "Jarvis, say happy birthday to [her name]"
> *JARVIS delivers a personalized birthday message*
> (Pre-programmed in the system prompt — make it sweet, make it you)

**Step 5: Hand it over**
Let her play with it. Suggest commands:
- "Hey Jarvis, change the lights to blue"
- "Hey Jarvis, play something chill"
- "Hey Jarvis, tell me a joke"
- "Hey Jarvis, what's the meaning of love?"

---

## Backup Plan

If anything fails during the demo:
- Music won't play → Have a Spotify/YouTube playlist ready on your phone
- Lights won't respond → Manually control via Tuya/Wipro app
- JARVIS crashes → "It's shy around new people" (buy time, restart the service via SSH on your phone)
- Wake word doesn't trigger → Move closer to the mic, speak clearly

---

## Notes
- Pre-craft the birthday message in the system prompt. Test it 10 times.
- Make sure the message doesn't sound generic. Include personal details.
- Charge the BT speaker to 100% the night before.
- Have the Jetson plugged into a stable power source (no extension cord chains).
